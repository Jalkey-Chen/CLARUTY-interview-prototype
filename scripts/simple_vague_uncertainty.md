# Simpler + Broad Uncertainty Script

## Prototype note

This is placeholder script content for the CLARITY interview prototype. It uses simpler wording and should be reviewed by a clinician before patient-facing use.

## Speaker 1 / Speaker 2 dialogue

### Opening

**Speaker 1:** This explanation gives the big picture from the note.

**Speaker 2:** It uses shorter sentences and focuses on the most important points.

### Main diagnosis

**Speaker 1:** The note says the diagnosis is metastatic thymic carcinoma.

**Speaker 2:** This means cancer from the thymus area has spread to other parts of the body.

### What the scans and biopsy showed

**Speaker 1:** The note describes cancer in the thymus area.

**Speaker 2:** It also mentions the liver, lymph nodes, bones, small lung nodules, and very small brain findings that need another look later.

### Current and recommended treatment

**Speaker 1:** Treatment has already started with carboplatin and paclitaxel.

**Speaker 2:** The note recommends carboplatin, paclitaxel, and ramucirumab, then repeat imaging after the first two cycles if appropriate.

### What is still uncertain

**Speaker 1:** A few findings still need follow-up.

**Speaker 2:** The care team may want more imaging or monitoring to understand what these findings mean.

### Questions to discuss with the care team

**Speaker 1:** You can ask what the treatment is meant to do and what side effects to watch for.

**Speaker 2:** You can also ask when the next scans will happen and when to call the care team.

### Closing

**Speaker 1:** This prototype is meant to support understanding.

**Speaker 2:** A clinician should review final content before it is used with patients.
